Case Study — 01

Reelify

Streaming Platform Mobile App Design

Reelify is a conceptual mobile streaming platform designed to enhance how users explore and consume entertainment content. The goal was to create a structured and engaging streaming experience that simplifies content discovery while maintaining a visually immersive interface.

Year

2024

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

The Challenge

  • Overwhelming content layout that causes decision fatigue
  • Lack of clear hierarchy between featured, trending, and recommended content
  • Navigation friction due to excessive scrolling
  • Minimal differentiation between personalized and general content

The Results

  • Delivered complete high-fidelity mobile prototype
  • Improved clarity in content discovery flow
  • Created immersive dark-mode streaming interface
  • Developed structured navigation system ready for usability testing

Smart Campus Support Platform

SUSKAID is a smart campus support platform designed to modernize student service interactions within the university ecosystem. As campus administrative services continue to rely on manual processes and fragmented communication channels, students often experience delays, unclear request tracking, and limited accessibility.

Year

2024

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

The Challenge

  • No real-time request tracking for student inquiries
  • Manual administrative workflow causing service delays
  • Limited accessibility for students with diverse needs

The Results

  • Developed a complete high-fidelity mobile prototype
  • Improved clarity in support request tracking
  • Designed accessible interaction through voice feature
  • Delivered structured support flow ready for implementation

Case Study — 03

Hair
Healthy

Hay — Beauty-Tech Mobile Application Design

Hay (Hair Healthy) is a beauty-tech mobile application designed to help users identify hair damage conditions and receive personalized care recommendations. Beyond improving user experience, the product also needed a sustainable monetization model to support professional consultation services.

Year

2024

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

The Challenge

  • Lack of structured hair diagnosis for accurate condition assessment
  • Generic product recommendations without personalization
  • Low user trust in self-diagnosis without professional validation
  • Need for a sustainable monetization model supporting consultations

The Results

  • Designed a guided hair condition assessment flow to improve diagnosis accuracy
  • Integrated subscription-based consultation with certified hair professionals
  • Created a tiered access model balancing free assessment and premium expert support
  • Improved perceived credibility by combining AI recommendations with human validation
  • Delivered a scalable beauty-tech concept aligned with FMCG digital transformation trends
